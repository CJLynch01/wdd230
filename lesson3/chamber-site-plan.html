Site Name

Washington Terrace Chamber of Commerce. I live in the city of Washington Terrace.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Washington Terrace Chamber of Commerce is to provide access to resources and relationships for local businesses to market their products. It adds credibility for local businesses through membership. The Chamber of Commerce is a valuable tool for small businesses.

Scenarios

What types of events does the Chamber of Commerce hold to incorporate businesses into the community? What kind of advertising comes with a Chamber of Commerce membership? Are there any group benefits offered through the Chamber of Commerce?
